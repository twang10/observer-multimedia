How does Tufts do long distance?

10% of Tufts students are currently in a long-distance relationship. The Tufts Observer polled nearly 300 people to see how we think, talk, fight, and love one another when we're miles, states, or oceans apart.

scroll down

The average distance separating us from our siginifcant others is 1,854 miles.

The farthest-separated couple is 12,000 miles apart.

18% of long-distance couples have spent nearly their entire relationship apart so far.

The longest-separated couple has been long-distance for 5.8 years.

The average long-distance relationship breaks up after 8.9 months of separation.

60.6% of respondants have had the legitimacy of their relationship questioned due to it being long-distance.

How do we communicate?

52% of respondants said their communication skills improved as a result of being in a long-distance relationship.
